Key Remapper

Connect your keyboard

Make sure the keyboard is powered on and in range.

  1. Turn on the keyboard
  2. Hold the top 3 keys on the left edge simultaneously until the LEDs light up — the keyboard is now discoverable. These keys send nothing while held.
  3. With your free hand, pair with your keyboard from your device's Bluetooth settings, then release the keys

Already paired? Skip straight to Connect — no combo needed.
